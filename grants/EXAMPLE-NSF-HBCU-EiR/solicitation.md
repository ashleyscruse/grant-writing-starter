## Summary (what you'd capture)

**Program:** NSF HBCU Excellence in Research (HBCU-EiR)
**Funder:** National Science Foundation
**Purpose:** Strengthen research capacity at HBCUs by funding PIs who have not recently held NSF research awards.
**Award:** up to ~$500K total, up to 3 years.
**Eligibility:** PI at an accredited HBCU; limits on recent NSF PI history.
**Review criteria:** Intellectual Merit and Broader Impacts (standard NSF), plus capacity-building at the HBCU.
**Key components:** Project Summary (1 p), Project Description (15 pp), Budget + Justification, Facilities & Resources, Biosketches, Data Management Plan.

For the real solicitation, see NSF's program page and the current NOFO.
