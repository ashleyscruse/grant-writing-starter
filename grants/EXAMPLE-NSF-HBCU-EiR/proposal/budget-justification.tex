\section*{Budget Justification}

\textbf{Senior Personnel.} The PI (Dr. Chen) requests one summer month per year to
lead method development and oversee all objectives. The Co-PI (Dr. Taylor, Spelman College)
requests one-half summer month per year to direct wet-lab validation.

\textbf{Other Personnel.} One postdoctoral researcher at 100\% effort supports the
computational pipeline. Two undergraduate researchers are supported during the
academic year and full-time in summer, advancing the broader-impacts plan.

\textbf{Fringe Benefits.} Calculated at the institution's negotiated rate of 30\% of
salaries and wages.

\textbf{Travel.} One conference per year for dissemination of results.

\textbf{Materials and Supplies.} Cloud and HPC compute credits and software licenses.

\textbf{Indirect Costs.} Applied per the institution's federally negotiated F\&A rate.
