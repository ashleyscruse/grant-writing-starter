# Alignment

Strategic context so your proposals align with the people and entities you have to work with: your institution's strategic plan, the funder's strategic plan, and federal or national priorities.

The AI reads this when it evaluates fit and when it drafts, so your framing speaks to what your institution and your funder are trying to achieve. Strong proposals mirror the funder's stated priorities and advance the institution's strategy; reviewers and program officers notice.

Fill these in once and reuse them on every proposal. Update when a new strategic plan comes out.

## Files

- `institution-strategy.md`: your institution's strategic plan priorities and initiatives.
- `funder-and-federal-priorities.md`: the funder's strategic plan, plus the federal and national priorities your work should speak to.

Drop the actual strategic-plan PDFs in this folder too if you have them; the AI will read them.
