# Profile

Your single profile. This is the knowledge base the AI reads for every grant.

You don't fill this in by hand. **Drop your CV in this folder** (`profile/me/cv.pdf`) and tell Sage "build my profile." Sage reads your CV into the sections below, then asks a few short questions a CV can't answer (your honest gaps and what you're chasing right now).

---

## Identity

Name, positions, education, research areas, technical skills.

## Research

Core research focus, active agenda, publications. (The most important section for fit analysis.)

## Teaching and Mentoring

Programs led, courses, mentoring counts and outcomes, outreach. (Drives NSF broader-impacts narratives.)

## Grant History

Grants and fellowships received, and any proposals in progress.

### Strengths

What makes you compelling to reviewers: published methods, preliminary data, resources, mentoring record, positioning.

### Honest Gaps

What's missing that would weaken a proposal. This is the most important section, and the one your CV will not have. Sage asks you for it.

### Current Priorities

What you are actively trying to fund right now: the program, agency, or deadline you are aiming at.
