# Research Profile

This is the most important profile document for grant analysis. The more specific you are about your research, the better Claude can match you to opportunities.

Fill this in directly (Sage can interview you and write your answers in for you). This file is `profile/you/research.md` — already where the AI looks for it.

## Core Research

Describe your primary research contribution in 2-4 sentences. Focus on: What is the key problem? What is your approach or method? What makes it different from other work in the field?

Using single-cell RNA-seq data and network-based approaches, I map regulatory pathways that are active in tumor microenvironments. This work bridges computational biology and health equity by focusing on cancers that are underrepresented in existing genomic databases.)*
