# Collaborators

The people you work with or plan to work with. One folder per person, built the same easy way as your own profile: from their CV.

**To add someone:** copy the `_TEMPLATE/` folder, rename it for the person (e.g. `taylor-james/`), drop their CV in it, and tell Sage **"add this person as a collaborator."** The `cv-import` skill reads the CV into their `profile.md` and asks you the two things a CV can't say: how you know them, and what they bring that you don't have.

```
collaborators/
  taylor-james/
    profile.md        built from their CV + your answers
    cv.pdf
```

When a grant needs expertise you don't have, the AI looks here to suggest team members and drafts personnel text from the real documents.
